THE PROCESS

TRANSPARENCY:

Why is there still a gender gap? What is preventing women, women of color, from joining the STEM fields, specifically in engineering and computer science?

OPPORTUNITY:

We want to create a website with resources, data visualizations, opportunities, and experiences to empower young women to enter the field.

ANALYSIS:

We saw many websites showing only the numbers of the gender gap in STEM. They only display the difference in numbers between men and women in the field. That's it.

STRATEGY:

Instead of just showing the numbers, we wanted to research and survey communities of young women of color to find out what is deterring them from STEM.

TRANSFORMATION:

We want to reach many communities of young women of color and make the reasons clear to why the gender gap is a major problem and why it still exists today.
